# Cover Letter Generator

## When to Use This Skill

Use when user wants to write a cover letter. Best results after job-description-analyzer gives you clear talking points.

## Cover Letter Structure

Length: 250-400 words (3-4 paragraphs). Tone: Confident, personalized, professional.

```
Opening Paragraph: Hook + Position + Why This Company (2-3 sentences)
Body Paragraph 1: Strongest qualification match (3-4 sentences)
Body Paragraph 2: Additional qualifications + address any gaps (3-4 sentences)
Closing Paragraph: Call to action + enthusiasm (2-3 sentences)
```

## Opening Paragraph Strategies

Pick ONE hook type:

1. **Specific Company Knowledge:** "I was excited to see [Company]'s recent [news/launch] — as a [role] who's spent [X] years in [area], I saw how my experience could [contribute]."

2. **Mutual Connection:** "[Name] on your [team] mentioned you're looking for [role]. Having worked with [Name] at [Company] and [relevant experience], I'd love to discuss..."

3. **Problem-Solver:** "Your JD mentions [challenge] — I've navigated this exact situation, [specific example with result]."

4. **Impressive Achievement:** "Last year, I [major achievement]. I'm excited to bring that [mindset/approach] to [Company]."

**Opening Don'ts:** Never start with "I am writing to apply for..." or "I am the perfect candidate...". Don't start with "I".

## Body Paragraph Frameworks

**Body 1 — Direct Match:** [Their Need] + [Your Exact Experience] + [Specific Result]

**Body 2 — Broader Value + Gap Handling:**
If you have gaps: "While my [skill] is developing ([action taken to improve]), I bring [related strength] demonstrated through [achievement]."
If no gaps: Add more value and company research.

## Closing Paragraph

Express enthusiasm for something specific → reference a contribution you'd make → call to action → thank them.

**Don'ts:** "I look forward to hearing from you" (passive). "Please find my resume attached" (obvious).

## Industry-Specific Considerations

- Tech/Engineering: Mention specific technologies, reference GitHub/portfolio, show you understand their tech stack
- Marketing/Creative: Reference their campaigns or brand voice, include relevant metrics
- Finance/Consulting: More formal tone, lead with credentials, emphasize analytical rigor
- Startup vs Enterprise: Startup = casual, scrappy, growth mindset; Enterprise = formal, process, scale

## Handling Scenarios

- Don't know hiring manager: "Dear Hiring Manager," or "Dear [Department] Team,"
- You have a referral: Lead with it immediately
- Underqualified: Emphasize transferable skills, quick learning, genuine enthusiasm
- Overqualified: Explain your motivation explicitly
- Career change: Connect old field to new through transferable skills

## Output Format

Provide:
1. Analysis Summary (match score, key strengths to highlight, gaps to address)
2. Full generated cover letter
3. Two alternative opening hooks
4. Key talking points for follow-up interview discussions

## Quality Checklist

1. Opens with a hook (not "I am writing to apply")
2. Mentions specific company knowledge
3. Connects experience directly to job requirements
4. Includes at least one specific metric/achievement
5. Addresses any obvious gaps
6. Has confident but not arrogant tone
7. Ends with clear call to action
8. Is 250-400 words
9. Contains no typos or grammatical errors
