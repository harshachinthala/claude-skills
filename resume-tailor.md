# Resume Tailor

## When to Use This Skill

Use after analyzing a job description. Tailor = select and highlight the most relevant true experiences — not fabrication.

## Tailoring Philosophy

You're not lying — you're highlighting the most relevant parts of your true experience. Think of your full experience as a library; tailoring means selecting the books that best fit what each employer is looking for.

## Tailoring Process

1. Analyze the job (use job-description-analyzer first)
2. Audit your resume — does each section support THIS specific role?
3. Make strategic adjustments:
   - Professional Summary: Rewrite to mirror job's key requirements
   - Skills: Reorder to put most relevant first; add missing keywords
   - Experience: Reorder jobs if less recent is more relevant; swap bullet order; add keywords naturally
   - Education: Highlight relevant coursework, certifications

## Section-by-Section Guide

### Professional Summary

Customize for each application. Mirror the job title and function, include top 5 keywords from JD, reference years of relevant experience.

### Skills Section Reordering

Put the skills most emphasized in the JD first. Add keywords from JD that you genuinely have.

### Experience Section

**Strategy 1: Reorder Jobs** — if less recent role is more relevant, move it up (keep dates).

**Strategy 2: Swap Bullet Order** — lead with bullets most relevant to target role (leadership vs. technical).

**Strategy 3: Adjust Bullet Language** — incorporate JD keywords while staying truthful.
```
JD says "stakeholder management" → Your bullet "Worked with teams" → Tailored: "Managed stakeholder relationships across 5 departments"
```

## Truth vs. Tailoring Line

**Acceptable:** Reordering true information, emphasizing relevant experience, using industry-standard terminology, adding context to vague statements.

**Unacceptable:** Adding skills you don't have, changing numbers/metrics, creating fake experiences, claiming titles you didn't hold.

## Version Management

- Keep ONE master resume as source of truth
- Name files: `LastName_Resume_TargetRole_Company_Date.pdf`
- Track which version went to which company (for interview prep)

## Quick Tailoring Checklist

1. Summary mentions the exact job title/function
2. Top 5 skills match JD's top 5 requirements
3. Most relevant experience positioned first
4. Each job's top bullet addresses job's key requirement
5. Keywords from JD appear naturally throughout
6. All claims are truthful
7. ATS formatting maintained
